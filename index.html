The 3D Game of Life

In the Game of Life, the population of cube evolves over time.

In each generation, whether a cube lives or dies depends on how many neighbors it has.

Here's the breakdown:

  1. If a cube has between and neighbors, it stays alive to the next generation. Otherwise it dies (by over- or underpopulation.)
  2. If an empty cell has between and neighbors, it becomes alive in the next generation (by reproduction).
